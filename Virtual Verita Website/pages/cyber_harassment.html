Cyber Harassment

Cyber-stalking or harassment can happen to anyone and there are several things you can do including report it to the police and possibly a lawsuit. If you don’t know who is stalking or harassing you, we can help you find out confidentially.

We can gather evidence in a court acceptable manner, create professional reports, and testify in court if necessary.

Cyber stalking and harassment causes a person to fear for their safety and may include such things as:

  • Distribution of personal sexual photographs or embarrassing photographs
  • Threatening violence or harm in insulting emails or text messages
  • Unwanted sexually explicit material in email, mail, or text messages
  • Making unreasonable or offensive demands, or ultimatums in email or text
  • Making statements that would cause a reasonable person to fear for their safety
  • Making discriminatory or derogatory remarks about sexual preference, race, sex, or religion
