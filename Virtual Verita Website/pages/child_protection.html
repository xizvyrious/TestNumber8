Child Protection Investigations

We are here to help you and your young person when you need it.

Do you suspect that something is wrong in your child’s life? Perhaps you already dismissed whatever it is to just growing pains. Well protecting children and giving them good advice is what good parents do. The problem is that young people can hide their depression, troubles, or anxiety from parents or shut parents out so they can’t help. We may be able to provide you the information you need to talk to your child, find your child, or prosecute an offender if necessary.

Does your child have a problem with Cyberbullying or Sexting?

According to www.bullyingstatistics.org

  • Over 50% of young people have been or engaged in cyberbullying others at some time.
  • The majority of young people being bullied are not likely to tell their parents
  • 33% of adolescents have experience online threats
  • 20% of minors have sent or received sexually suggestive or nude selfies
  • 10% of minors have had embarrassing or damaging photos taken without permissions circulated online
  • 80% of adolescents use smart phones; the most common device used for bullying



Did your child run away, leave suddenly, or was taken by a relative or spouse?

We may be able to help you locate your child, and you have a good reason to be concerned. Many runaways end up on the streets and are exploited and abused by sex traffickers.

According to the 2013 Annual Report of the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children NCMEC

  • 10,094 children were reported missing in 2013
  • 14.3% of endangered runaway children reported were identified as likely victims of child sex trafficking
