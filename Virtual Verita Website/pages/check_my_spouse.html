Check My Spouse
There are many different reasons why digital forensics can be very helpful in spousal investigations. You should know that the state of Michigan is a no-fault divorce state and infidelity does not have to be proven. However, you might consider digital forensics for several other reasons besides infidelity.
We can examine and extract evidence from computers and smart phones to help you:
  • Determine if your spouse has accessed your email or other files.
  • Determine if your spouse has bugged your computer or smart phone.
  • Assess the possibility of the infidelity and cyber-sexual activity.
  • Gather evidence of abuse, neglect, harassment, theft, or unlawful activity to be provided to police from your smart phone or shared computer.
  • Review Internet use and search activity including the applications used.
  • Locate a missing spouse using digital evidence from a shared computer or smart phone.
