DMG = ATK * (1 + Sphere + LS1 + LS2 + P + O + aBB + DM) * C * EW * Sparks

ATK = Attack Statistic of Unit being calculated (REQUIRED)
Sphere = summation of Sphere 1 & Sphere 2 equipped (ATK Bonus Only)
LS1 = Your Leader Skill (ATK Bonus Only)
LS2 = Friend Leader Skill (ATK Bonus Only)
P = Elixir (ATK Bonus Only)
O = Ore (ATK Bonus Only)
aBB = summation of Attack boost related to Brave Burst for each applicable unit
DM = Damage Multiplier given by BB/SBB of Unit being calculated
C = summation of Critical Hit Damage modifiers
EW = summation of Elemental Weakness modifiers
Sparks = Sparks (Omitted for the time being, situational/complex per possible spark)

* If there are no applicable bonuses, leave field(s) set to 0

* All values are to be entered as decimal format,
convert % to 0.00, ex: 115% = 1.15 or 70% = 0.70
